CoreMotion – The Online Physiotherapist

The Doubt Everyone Has

“Can you really treat someone without touching them?” — It’s a fair concern. Physiotherapy is about movement science — understanding how your body moves, finding what’s restricted, and guiding you to restore balance and control. Online physiotherapy brings that same level of care into your home via secure video consultation.

The Real Power of Connection

Being in your own space makes sessions more personal. You feel comfortable, focused, and connected, allowing better communication and trust. CoreMotion listens to your story, habits, and pain to create a program that fits your lifestyle and lasts.

The Science Says Yes

Research shows online physiotherapy is as effective as in-person care for most musculoskeletal conditions. Patients who receive virtual care stay consistent, attend follow-ups, and complete exercises, driving lasting recovery.

✨ Research Evidence
  • Cottrell MA et al., Clinical Rehabilitation, 2017.
  • Grona SL et al., Journal of Telemedicine and Telecare, 2018.
