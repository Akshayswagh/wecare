How Online Physiotherapy Works

A simple, evidence-based process that brings expert physiotherapy straight to your space — clear, personal, and completely human.

A New Way to Begin Recovery

If you’ve never experienced online physiotherapy before, it can sound unfamiliar — maybe even a little futuristic. But in reality, it’s beautifully simple. At CoreMotion, we’ve designed a process that feels natural, structured, and deeply personal. It’s not about technology replacing care; it’s about using technology to make better care possible. You don’t need complex software or fancy equipment — just a smartphone, tablet, or laptop, and a little space to move.

The First Step: Understanding You

Every journey starts with a conversation. During your first session, your physiotherapist listens carefully to your story — how your pain began, how it feels, and how it impacts your day-to-day life. You’ll be guided through a few simple movements on camera. This helps your therapist assess your mobility, strength, and posture — all from the screen. You might be surprised how much a trained physiotherapist can notice through observation: A subtle shoulder lift, a hip shift, or the way you bend to one side — these clues reveal what’s really happening beneath the surface. Our goal during this session is simple: understand before we treat.

Personalised Care, Powered by Innovation

Once your assessment is complete, your therapist designs a custom program — clear, structured, and completely tailored to your goals. Your program includes:

  • Demonstration videos of each exercise.
  • Written instructions to ensure perfect form.
  • Progress tracking and reminders through our innovative software.

This approach ensures your care continues beyond each session. You can practice your exercises safely at home, track your improvements, and message your therapist for guidance whenever needed. You’re never alone in your recovery — you’re supported every step of the way.

Progress That Feels Personal

Follow-up sessions are designed to keep your progress steady and sustainable. Your physiotherapist reviews your movement, fine-tunes your exercises, and gradually increases your challenge level. We celebrate the small wins — improved mobility, less stiffness, more confidence — because these are the milestones that lead to lasting change. What makes online physiotherapy unique is its flexibility. You can continue your program while travelling, at home, or during a busy week — without missing a session. The process fits you, not the other way around.

Why It Works

The success of online physiotherapy lies in its partnership. It’s not a passive treatment — it’s an active collaboration between you and your therapist. Every exercise, every cue, every conversation is designed to help you understand your body better. And the more awareness you develop, the faster and more confidently you recover. It’s this combination — of expert guidance, patient education, and consistent movement — that makes online physiotherapy so effective.

Research-Backed Results

Studies have shown that video-based physiotherapy assessments are highly valid and reliable for most conditions, including back pain, shoulder issues, and knee injuries. Research from the Journal of Telemedicine and Telecare and Clinical Rehabilitation confirms that online physiotherapy delivers the same recovery outcomes as in-person sessions — often with greater convenience and long-term adherence.

Research Evidence

  • Mani S et al., Journal of Telemedicine and Telecare, 2017.
  • Cottrell MA et al., Clinical Rehabilitation, 2017.

At CoreMotion, we believe recovery should never feel distant. Through expert care, innovative tools, and genuine connection, we bring world-class physiotherapy to your home — wherever you are. Movement. Guidance. Progress — all within reach.
