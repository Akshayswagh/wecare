CoreMotion – The Online Physiotherapist

A New Way to Begin Recovery

Online physiotherapy brings expert care to your space — clear, personal, and human. All you need is a smartphone, tablet, or laptop and a little space to move.

The First Step: Understanding You

In your first session, your physiotherapist listens carefully to your story and guides you through simple movements to assess mobility, strength, and posture — all via video.

Personalised Care, Powered by Innovation

Based on your assessment, a custom program is designed with videos, instructions, and progress tracking. You’re supported safely at home, with the therapist available for guidance whenever needed.

Progress That Feels Personal

Follow-ups review your movement, fine-tune exercises, and increase challenge gradually. Online physiotherapy offers flexibility — you can continue your program anytime, anywhere.

Research-Backed Results

Studies confirm that video-based physiotherapy is valid and reliable, delivering outcomes equal to in-person care.
