About Us

WeCare The Online Physiotherapis.

At CoreMotion – The Online Physiotherapist, we believe recovery is more than exercises — it’s about understanding your body, building confidence, and living a stronger lifestyle. Physiotherapy here is clear, approachable, and empowering, helping you heal today while giving you the tools to stay active, independent, and pain-free tomorrow.

We’ve especially seen a rise in young professionals experiencing chronic pain—particularly in the neck and lower back—caused by long hours of desk work and poor ergonomics, preventable isn’t it. This not only affects their physical health but also impacts productivity and overall quality of life.

Our team consists of expert physiotherapists, each specialising in different areas of the body, ensuring that you receive focused, evidence-based care tailored to your condition.

Founder: Dr Aabha Ingle (PT) BPT, MSc (Advanced Physiotherapy, University of Birmingham, UK).

As leaders in virtual physiotherapy, we offer a level of expertise that sets us apart:

Our extensive clinical experience helps us quickly identify common patterns of injury recovery.

We provide evidence-informed advice and interventions that make a measurable difference.

We ask the right clinical questions to accurately assess your condition.

At WeCare The Online Physiotherapist, we’re committed to helping you regain movement, reduce pain, and restore function—all from the comfort of your own space.
