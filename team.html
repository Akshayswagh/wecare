Aabha Abhay Ingle

Founder, CoreMotion – The Online Physiotherapist

Meet Our Founder

I’m Aabha, a physiotherapist with a passion for making movement, recovery, and wellness simple and accessible for everyone.

My journey began with a strong academic foundation in physiotherapy, where I trained in advanced musculoskeletal and rehabilitation practices. This was complemented by valuable experience working with UK-based physiotherapy experts, allowing me to learn evidence-based approaches and patient-centered care. This international exposure shaped my vision for creating a modern platform that combines clinical precision with education and accessibility.

The Challenge I Saw

During my professional journey, I noticed a recurring pattern that inspired the creation of CoreMotion:

Unnecessary Pain

Too many people lived with chronic or acute pain that could be alleviated with proper intervention.

Myths & Misinformation

Confusion reigned due to widespread myths and a lack of clear, expert-backed information.

Limited Access

Access to expert physiotherapy advice often felt complicated, expensive, or simply out of reach for many.

My Vision for CoreMotion

I wanted to change that. That’s why I founded CoreMotion — a platform where physiotherapy is not just about exercises, but about education, lifestyle, and empowerment.

Our Mission:

  • To help people understand their body better.
  • To recover smarter and more efficiently.
  • To build the confidence needed to live pain-free and independent lives.

Beyond Clinical Work: My Passions

Outside of direct clinical practice, my commitment to holistic wellness drives me to explore related fields:

Brain & Lifestyle Science

Exploring how habits, mindset, and natural healing mechanisms profoundly affect overall health and recovery.

Digital Health Innovation

Leveraging cutting-edge technology to make expert physiotherapy guidance more accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Holistic Wellbeing

Advocating for the integration of exercise, breathwork, and conscious lifestyle choices for sustainable, long-term health impact.

For me, physiotherapy isn’t only a profession — it’s a mission to create a movement for movement.
